WiM Inspirational Leadership Legacy

WILL Program - MICCAI 2021

Application

Overview

WiM Inspirational Leadership legacy (WILL) program will be launched with a goal to enhance and spotlight our leaders and to build our community legacy. Participants will share video interviews with inspirational leaders (e.g., supervisor, colleague, etc) in their fields, walking the audience through the academic journey of the interviewee. An anonymous panel of judges will first review and shortlist the 10 best videos based on the content and interviewer’s skills. Based on public votes on these 10 shortlisted videos, we will give an equal award of $500 to the interviewers of the top 3 videos. All received videos will be uploaded to the WiM YouTube channel under a new “WILL 2021” playlist. WILL will be a recursive program which will take place every year and will be associated with WiM.

Guidelines/ Rules

  • The program is open to everyone (students, postdocs, early career researchers, academics) regardless of their career stage.
  • Participants are allowed to make a team of up to 3 members who will work together towards preparing and conducting an interview with their inspirational leader.
  • A team can have more than one entry; i.e. more than one interview
  • Each submission should be no less than 10 minutes in duration. Maximum allowed duration is 25 minutes.
  • The awards will only be for the participating team and not for the interviewee.

Prizes

Three prizes of $500 each will be awarded to the top three participants/teams through public voting.

Submission

Submission will be via email: submission@email.com

Important dates

  • Submission deadline: August 10, 2021
  • Announcement of the winners: MICCAI awards ceremony
