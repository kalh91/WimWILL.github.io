WiM Inspirational Leadership Legacy

WILL Program - MICCAI 2021

Evaluation Criteria

Aspects to cover in the interview:

  • An advice from the heart that the interviewee give to the early-career peers.​
  • The struggles/pumps they faced at the beginning of their careers, and how it turned to be a motivational story (whether personal for working mothers if applicable or career-wise).​
  • Sharing success stories/struggles with graduate students and post-docs on publishing manuscripts and/or grant writing.
​

Interviewer skills: ​ ​

  • Confidence
  • Preparation
  • Video Quality

Evaluation

  • Anonymous panel of judges will first review and shortlist the 5 best videos based on the content and interviewer’s skills​.
  • Based on public votes on the 5 shortlisted videos, equal award of $500 to be given to the interviewers of the top 3 videos.

Submission

Submission will be via email: submission@email.com

Important dates

  • Submission deadline: August 10, 2021
  • Announcement of the winners: MICCAI awards ceremony
